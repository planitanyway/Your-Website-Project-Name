I have always loved planning and organizing but more importantly, I enjoy helping people. I do this by creating simple strategies to bring an event together, developing a fitness plan to help people get back on track or providing accountability for their hard-earned life goals to be met.

I started Plan It Anyway as a way to connect with people, understand their struggles, and create a simple solution by sorting out what's important to the client and eliminating unnecessary distractions.

I have this saying when things seem impossible, "When in doubt, plan it out! Not sure how to slay the day, Plan It Anyway!"
